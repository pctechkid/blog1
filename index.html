We move forward, we find hope, and we find ways to adapt to any situation. Thus, MADE4Learners Framework was made.

MY PERSONAL ACCOUNTS

Finally! I got my personal accounts for Moodle & Office 365. Yohoooo! And oh, during this week I did make my personal zoom account too for me to get ready for my video conferences.
